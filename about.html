OUR JOURNEY

Super Drive was established on 30th April 2005 at Suriya with a clear vision to redefine the car showroom experience. From the very beginning, our goal has been to provide customers with high-quality vehicles and a smooth, reliable buying journey. Over the years, Super Drive has grown into a trusted name in the automobile industry by focusing on customer satisfaction, transparency, and excellence. We offer a wide range of vehicles to suit different needs and budgets, ensuring that every customer finds the perfect match. At Super Drive, we believe that buying a car should be exciting and stress-free. That’s why we provide personalized assistance, easy test drive options, and complete support from selection to purchase. Drive your dream with confidence — choose Super Drive.

Driving Trust

* Welcome to Super Drive, your ultimate destination for a premium multi-brand car buying experience. We proudly bring together a wide range of vehicles from all leading automobile brands under one roof, making it simple and convenient for customers to explore, compare, and choose their perfect car.

* Headquartered in Chennai, Super Drive is designed with world-class facilities to ensure a smooth and comfortable journey for every customer. Our showroom offers expert guidance, test drive options, finance and insurance assistance, and complete after-sales support — all in one place.

* With a strong presence across South India, we have successfully expanded our network with branches in Bangalore, Tenkasi, Coimbatore (Kovai), and Madurai. Our goal is to provide consistent, high-quality service and build long-lasting relationships with our customers in every location we serve.

* At Super Drive, our mission is to deliver a transparent, reliable, and customer-friendly car buying experience by offering multiple brand choices, genuine pricing, and professional support. We aim to make car ownership easier, smarter, and more enjoyable for everyone.

* Our vision is to become South India’s most trusted and preferred multi-brand car showroom by continuously improving our services, embracing innovation, and exceeding customer expectations.

* We offer a wide range of services including new car sales from all major brands, test drive bookings, easy finance options, insurance support, vehicle exchange services, and dedicated after-sales assistance. Our experienced team is always ready to guide customers in making the right choice based on their needs and preferences.

* Why choose Super Drive? Because we combine variety, trust, and convenience. With multiple brands under one roof, transparent processes, expert staff, and a customer-first approach, we ensure a hassle-free and premium experience every time you visit us.

* At Super Drive, we don’t just sell cars — we help you drive your dreams with confidence, comfort, and style.
